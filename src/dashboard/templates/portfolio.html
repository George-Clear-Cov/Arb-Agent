How fees work in this agent

Polymarket taker — 2% on net winnings. Use limit orders (maker) for 0% fee. The detector shows both gross and net margins.

Kalshi — ceil(0.07 × price × (1−price)) per contract added to cost basis. Approx 1–2% depending on contract price.

Betfair — 5% commission on net winnings from back bets; back/lay arbs shown separately.

Sportsbooks (Odds API) — vig baked into quoted odds. No additional fee modelled.

The net margin column in the Arb table is what you actually keep after all fees. Only act on net margins above ~2%.
